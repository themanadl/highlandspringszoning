Petition REZ2025-001 – Oakfield Road Rezoning

This petition addresses Case REZ2025-001, which proposes rezoning 66 acres along Oakfield Road from agricultural (A-1) to suburban residential (SR-1), allowing 2.4 dwellings per acre. Half of this land is wetlands, and increased development threatens to overburden local infrastructure and degrade environmental resources. We urge the Board of Supervisors to reject this request and preserve the current zoning and community character.
