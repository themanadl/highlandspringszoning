Petition Proposal: Baker Development Resources

(Proposal from the May 13, 2025, Meeting)

Baker Development Resources is proposing to rezone 700 North Airport Drive A-1 farmland and C-1 Conservatio District to R-5AC General Residential; the actual rezoning designation may change since this is a proposal. Baker is currently planning for high density housing at 700 North Airport Drive. The last proposal at the May 13, 2025 revealed that Baker intends to now build 50 high density, single family homes, and convert the community into an HOA. They are proposing one entrance/exit along North Airport Drive right next to the I-295 exit ramp. We cannot allow another community development to endanger current residents and invade our peaceful agricultural communities!
